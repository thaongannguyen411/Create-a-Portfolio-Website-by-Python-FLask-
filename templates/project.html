What is Home Base?


It is an mobile application for people (over18) who want to study in Sydney and need finding a place to stay during their course.


This app is designed into finding a 'house' in an easy and convenient way for international student.


Home Base will provide the transparent information of both host and guest on its platform so they can trust each other.



How Home Base come about?


In 2017, 799,371 international students were enrolled in education programs in Australia so they will increase the requirement of renting a house or room in Australia (Good Education Group 2017).


Nowadays, International students don't have an app or a clear website to find a place to stay so they are very hard to find safe place to stay during their course (ABC 2019).



What Home Base can do?


We wanted to make a friendlyand more comfortable app for students who are wanting to study in Sydney.


Make a place where hosts feel comfortable sharing or lending their home to students (aged 18-30) for longer time.


This app is for students aged 18 and older (Students under 18 need to have a legal guardian before entering Australia).
